About Me

From building frameworks to building businesses

I started out creating open-source tools like LIPS.js, Kenx, and Cubic Studio because I wanted to make development easier for everyone. That same drive led me to start building companies that solve real problems.

These days, I'm focused on mobility and supply chain tech. With De., I'm building a platform that makes logistics actually work the way it should. Roll Mobility brings e-scooters to university campuses in Ghana, and Mijo Ride is coming soon to make getting around easier for everyone.

I'm based in Accra, Ghana, and I still love contributing to open source when I can. It keeps me grounded and connected to the developer community that helped me get here.
